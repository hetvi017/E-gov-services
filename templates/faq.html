Frequently Asked Questions

What is Krishi E-Government Services? ▼

Krishi E-Government Services is an online platform that provides easy access to various government-related services such as PAN, Aadhaar, and other official documents, all in one place.

How can I apply for a government service? ▼

You can register or log in to your account, navigate to the “Services” page, choose the required service, fill in the necessary details, upload the required documents, and submit the form.

How can I track my application status? ▼

Once logged in, go to the “Application Tracking” section. You can view the progress of your submitted application under statuses such as “Pending,” “Processing,” or “Completed.”

How will I receive my documents? ▼

After your application is marked “Completed,” you can download your document directly from your dashboard. Additionally, a copy will be sent to your registered email address.

Who can I contact for help or support? ▼

You can reach out to our support team via the “Contact” page. Fill in the form or email us at support@krishiservices.in.
